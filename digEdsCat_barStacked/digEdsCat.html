Catalogue of Digital Editions

This stacked bar chart illustrates the distribution of digital editions collected in Greta Franzini's Catalogue of Digital Editions. Coloured bars represent the languages of the source texts (abbreviated here to their ISO codes). Hovering over any one bar will reveal the exact number of digital editions per time period and language.
'MIX' contains digital editions of Welsh, Mayan, Aramaic and other languages. As the data so far collected for these is very small and to avoid cluttering the visualisation, I've temporarily catalogued them under a single category.
To exclude periods from the visualisation, simply click on the legend item you wish to exclude.
