Catalogue of Digital Editions

This simple stacked bar chart illustrates the distribution of digital editions collected in my Catalogue of Digital Editions. Coloured bars represent the languages of the source texts (abbreviated here to their ISO codes). Hovering over any one bar will reveal the exact number of digital editions per time-period and language.
Language groups (e.g. ENM/ANG/SGA) were created on the basis of geographic vicinity and/or language similarity. 'MIX' contains digital editions of Welsh, Mayan, Aramaic and other (currently) under-represented languages. The data for these groups is currently so small (as low as 1 edition per language) that creating separate entries would only clutter the visualisation. As the data grows, I hope to be able to create one entry for each and every language.
To exclude time-periods from the visualisation, simply click on the legend item you wish to exclude.

N.B.. This bar chart displays only data that has been catalogued by either myself or previous contributors. Many projects still contain empty fields so this visualisation isn't a complete view of all editions present in the Catalogue. While I regularly update empty fields, some information is just not available for me to record.
Other visualisations are planned to illustrate the many other facets of the Catalogue.

Comments and suggestions to this chart are very welcome! Please leave your messages as "issues" in the Catalogue's GitHub repository and I'll get back to you. Grazie!
